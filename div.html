EN.520.345 Electrical & Computer Engineering Laboratory
This course consists of 11 one-week laboratory experiments intended to provide an introduction to analog and digital circuits commonly used in engineering. Topics include phase and frequency response, transistors, operational amplifiers, filters, and other analog circuits.The experiments are done using computer controlled digital oscilloscopes, function generators, and power supplies.Electrical & Computer Engineering Laboratory
